Award winning Full service agency

Titan Enterprises (QLD) Pty Ltd is one of Australia's great success stories. From humble beginnings the company has grown to be one of the largest and most successful steel building manufacturers in the country.

Titan were the first to use the Henrob self piercing rivet system that has received world-wide acclaim for its strength and design benefits. This remarkable method of joining steel members is stronger than welding, leaves the protective galvanized finish fully intact and does not distort or weaken the original metal.

Titan Garages and Sheds are continuously developing and improving Titan's range of buildings from simple Toolsheds to Heavy Commercial Industrial Buildings. We Have supplied Queensland and New South Wales with 20 years of Domestic, Commercial and Industrial Steel Building construction, At Titan we have a solution to all your Shed and storage needs.
